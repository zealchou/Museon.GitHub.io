AI Agents Shouldn't Fight Alone: The Power of External Leverage

When designing AI Agents, many people fall into a trap: stuffing all capabilities inside the Agent itself.

From my own practical experience, I've learned something: An Agent's real breakthrough often comes not from internal capability improvements, but from introducing external resources.
